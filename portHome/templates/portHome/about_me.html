About Me

My name is Simon Leigh. Originally from Newcastle, England, I have always taken and unconventional route through life in order to keep challenging myself.
After years of high level sports I needed a fresh challenge and choose to study Japanese in university. Two years in I took a gap-year during which time I moved to Japan and worked teaching English first in Shizuoka before moving to Kawasaki, Kanagawa for most of the year.
I then came to Akita International University where I transferred into their Global Studies program, mainly studying Political Science with a peripheral focus on media.
I have been programming as a hobby for a couple of years in the little free time I have. Managing to become relatively proficient in Python and Javascript along with a pretty successful foray into C#.
I still have much to learn and am always looking to take my skills to the next level.
